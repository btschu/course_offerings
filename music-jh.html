Band

Course Course # Grade Level Credit Course Description School Additional Information
Beginning Band 2612000 7, 8 1 This course is open to all 7th and 8th grade students who desire to develop the ability to play a brass or woodwind instrument and have never played an instrument or have limited playing experience. Students will learn the fundamentals of caring for the instrument, music reading for the instrument being studied including basic major scales, theory, rudiments, and fingerings. Enrollment in this band is also available to those students desiring to learn a different or additional band instrument. No previous band experience is necessary for enrollment in this course. Instruments taught include flute, clarinet, saxophone, oboe, bassoon, trumpet, horn, trombone, baritone, tuba. The course is considered a preparatory course for progression into Intermediate or Advanced Band. Additional Information
Percussion Band 2612350 7, 8 1 This course is open to all 7th and 8th grade students. This is the primary band for incoming 7th grade and continuing 8th grade percussionists. Students will development and study technique, theory, listening, and fundamentals of playing. This band performs additional styles, articulations, and literature. One to two years' previous band experience is typical for enrollment in this course however beginners may enroll. Rehearsals and performances that take place during co-curricular times (outside of the school day) are an expected component of this course. Attendance is required. The importance of sustained effort and practice is stressed for technical proficiency. This course may be repeated. Additional Information
Intermediate Band 2612200 7, 8 1 This course is open to all 7th and select 8th grade students who have achieved beyond the beginning band level and/or a minimum of one year previous instruction. This is the primary band for most incoming 7th graders. Students will continue their development and study of technique, theory, listening, and fundamentals of playing. This band performs a variety of styles and literature. One to two years' previous band experience is expected for enrollment in this course. Rehearsals and performances that take place during co-curricular times (outside of the school day) are an expected component of this course. Attendance is required. The importance of sustained effort and practice is stressed for technical proficiency. This course may be repeated. Additional Information
Advanced Band 2612400 7, 8 1 This course is open to 8th grade and select 7th grade students with at least two years previous band instruction. This is the primary band for most 8th graders. Course includes more advanced rehearsal and playing techniques and performance literature. Students will continue their study of theory, scales, rhythms, technique and practice habits in preparation for Wind Ensemble. Incoming seventh grade musicians will be placed in this band by audition or director recommendation. Rehearsals and performances that take place during co-curricular times (outside of the school day) are an expected component of this course. Attendance is required. The importance of sustained effort and practice is stressed for technical proficiency. This course may be repeated. Additional Information
