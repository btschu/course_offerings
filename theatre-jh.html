Junior High Theatre

Course Course # Grade Level Credit Course Description School Additional Information
Exploratory Theatre Arts 2499997 7 1/4 This is a 9-week elective course and part of the 7th Grade Elective Wheel. This introductory course lays the foundation for understanding a variety of theatre skills such as stage movements, acting, and improvisation.
Intro to Theatre Arts 2420100 8 1/2 This elective course explores a variety of technical and performance skills: Design, script analysis, stage movement, acting, careers and theatre history. Activities can include: script analysis; design projects such as props, set, costume, make-up; and scene work. This course will not include performances outside of the classroom.
Theatre Production 2420300 8 1/2 This elective course develops theatre skills through performance. Students will participate in set construction, costuming, stage movement, props, make-up, sound, publicity, and acting through a culminating production for a live audience. Teacher Approval Required.
