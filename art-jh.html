Juinior High Art

Course Course # Grade Level Credit Course Description School Additional Information
Art 1 - 7A Elective Wheel 2000700 7 1/4 Students will have the opportunity to increase their understanding and appreciation of Art through responding to and making two-dimensional visual images using a variety of materials, processes, and art making approaches; such as drawing, painting, printmaking and graphic design. Students will develop their creativity, problem solving, and observation skills by creating their own pieces inspired by different art movements, cultures, and processes. Students will develop an artistic empathy by interpreting artwork presented by others, as well as sharing and presenting. Students will be required to keep a sketchbook and develop a portfolio of work.
Art 2D 2000900 8 1/2 Students in 2D Art will build their artistic development to create original artworks, while exploring a variety of 2-dimensional media; such as drawing, painting, printmaking, and graphic design. Students will develop an understanding and empathy for art by learning to analyze, interpret, and present their own 2-dimensional artwork. Students will push themselves to develop their own personal narratives in their artwork through reflection. Students will be required to keep a sketchbook and develop a portfolio of artwork.
Art 3D 2090000 8 1/2 Students in Art 3D will build their artistic development to create original artworks while exploring a variety of 3-dimensional media; such as sculpture, techniques in ceramics, paper art, printmaking, fibers, and beading. Students will develop an understanding and empathy for art by learning to analyze, interpret, and present their own 3-dimensional artwork. Students will push themselves to develop their own personal narratives in their artwork through reflection. Students will be required to keep a sketchbook and develop a portfolio of artwork.
